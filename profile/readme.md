# Solar Bear

A hybrid Sun/muscle-powered trike!

The purpose of the __Solar Bear__ is to:

- Transport people inside cities,
- Reduce the gas emissions,
- Reduce noise inside cities,
- Consume electrical energy where it is produced,
- Use solar panels as trike's body,
- Be safer than a bike,
- Occupy less parking space than a car,
- Motivate people to exercise,
- Motivate people to build their own trike.

# Technical specs

- recumbent trike,
- 2.5m long, 1.45m wide (including wheels),
- weight: 70kg (excluding driver, including panels),
- assisted pedalling,
- motor max speed: 25km/h,
- motor (continuous) power: 250W,
- solar power: max 400W,
- battery: owner choice.

## Build instructions

- Cut the bars to required size and angle,
- Build (weld) the structure,
- Reinforce solar panels,
- Place solar panels,
- Connect eletrical installation,
- Go!
